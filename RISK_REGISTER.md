# Risk Register

## Launch Risks

| Risk | Impact | Current handling | Before launch |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `RESEND_API_KEY` or `RESEND_FROM_EMAIL` is not configured. | Customers may not receive £49 report-ready emails or £249 Senior Finance Review confirmation emails. | Payment confirmation, report viewing and senior review confirmation still work. The server logs safe diagnostics and does not expose provider errors. | Configure `RESEND_API_KEY` and `RESEND_FROM_EMAIL`, then test customer emails for both packages. |
| `INTERNAL_REVIEW_EMAIL` is not configured. | A £249 order may be paid and confirmed, but no internal review notification will be sent. | The customer still sees the senior review confirmation state. The server logs a safe diagnostic. | Configure `INTERNAL_REVIEW_EMAIL`, then test a paid `senior-finance-review` order end to end. |
| `REPORT_LINK_SECRET` is not configured in production. | Signed report links should not be considered launch-ready. | Local development can use a development fallback. Production does not issue valid signed report access without the secret. | Configure `REPORT_LINK_SECRET` in production and verify signed £49 report links before launch. |
| Scanned, image-only, password-protected or unusually formatted PDFs may not extract usable text. | The intake may reject the upload unless the customer also pastes CV text or provides a text-based file. | The order creation route gives customer-safe guidance and does not create an order without usable CV evidence. | Test representative PDF, DOCX and TXT CVs, including at least one scanned PDF failure case. |
| Vercel Function request payloads have a platform limit. | Large CV uploads could fail before the order is created. | The app limits CV uploads to 4MB, below the documented Vercel Function request body limit. | Test PDF, DOCX and TXT uploads close to 4MB in the staging deployment. |
| AI output quality can vary by role, CV evidence quality and target job advert detail. | A paid report may be less commercially sharp if the input evidence is thin. | The prompt instructs the model to avoid fabrication and the deterministic fallback keeps generation available. | Manually review a sample of real Gemini-generated reports across Finance Manager, FP&A, Head of Finance and Finance Director use cases. |
| Legal, privacy and refund wording is launch-draft only. | Public trust pages may need professional legal review before live traffic. | Privacy, Terms and Refund Policy pages exist and avoid outcome guarantees. | Complete legal/privacy review, including CV storage, AI processing, signed report links, email delivery and data retention. |
| Duplicate Stripe success/webhook handling may trigger repeated Senior Finance Review emails in rare timing windows. | A customer or internal reviewer could receive duplicate confirmation messages. | Both paths are server-side and order status is checked before sending. | Test webhook and success-page timing in Stripe test mode; consider an email-sent audit field before launch scale. |
